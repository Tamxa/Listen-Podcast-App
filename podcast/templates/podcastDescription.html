How I Built This

TRAJ161

Author: Guy Raz

Language: English

About: How I Built This is a captivating podcast hosted by the award-winning journalist Guy Raz, who delves into the stories behind the people who created some of the world's best-known companies. Each episode offers an in-depth interview with a successful entrepreneur or innovator, exploring the journey from the initial spark of an idea to the establishment of a thriving business. Listeners gain insights into the challenges, failures, and triumphs faced along the way, as well as the strategies and mindsets that drove these individuals to success. Featuring a diverse range of industries and backgrounds, "How I Built This" inspires and educates aspiring entrepreneurs and curious minds alike. Tune in to hear the remarkable tales of perseverance, creativity, and innovation that turn dreams into reality.
